Our Mission

Who We Are

"To increase the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community."

Founded nationally in 1975, NSBE is one of the largest student-governed organizations in the United States. The UNL chapter serves Black engineering students at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln through programming, mentorship, professional development, and community engagement.

The NSBE Annual Convention (NSBEAC) is the largest gathering of Black engineers in the world — a career fair, technical workshops, networking, and competitions. We send UNL members every year.
